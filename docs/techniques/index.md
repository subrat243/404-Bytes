# Techniques

A structured collection of offensive and defensive cybersecurity techniques used across different phases of penetration testing. Covers methods for reconnaissance, enumeration, exploitation, privilege escalation, persistence, and evasion. Includes step-by-step approaches, real-world scenarios, and practical examples such as XSS, SQL Injection, IDOR, SSRF, authentication bypass, and network attacks.

Focuses on the `methodology` behind attacks rather than just tools—explaining how vulnerabilities are discovered, analyzed, and exploited in real environments.

**Purpose**: To develop a deep understanding of how attacks actually work, enabling you to think like an attacker, identify weaknesses efficiently, and apply the right technique in the right situation.
